Murphy

Murphy is the only kitten we have fostered and required the most effort from us. When we received him, he was a 8 week old feral kitten who had had almost no human contact. We had to slowly work with him over several weeks to build up his trust in humans. As you can see from the photos below once he trusted us, he couldn't stay away from us. Always wanting more cuddles.
