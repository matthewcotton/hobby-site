Jenny & Matt's Foster Cats

Over the last 3 years Jenny and I have been a foster home for eight of Toronto Cat Rescue's cats. Toronto Cat Rescue is a registered, no-kill charity. With a mission is to rescue and find homes for abandoned, sick or injured cats from situations of abuse, neglect or imminent euthanasia. And a vision for Toronto to be a city in which no adoptable cat is killed and rescue organizations such as Toronto Cat Rescue are no longer needed. This site includes a brief description of each cat and a selection of photos. The site is The Developer Academy's week 2 project.
