Dad

You may think that Dad is an odd name for a cat but there is a tragic reason behind it. He was found tied in a bag with several kittens. We do not know if he was these kitten's dad but as he was the only adult he gained the name "Dad". Given this tragic ordeal he was very timid and hid from us for days. We gave him time and space which allowed him grow in confidence. After a few weeks he became one of the friendliest we have fostered.
